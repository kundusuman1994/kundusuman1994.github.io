Research

My work sits at the boundary of theoretical and computational high energy physics. I am broadly interested in understanding the fundamental structure of matter and forces through precision calculations and new physics searches.

Collider Phenomenology

Precision QCD calculations, Monte Carlo methods, and jet physics for current and future collider experiments.

Beyond the Standard Model

New physics signatures at the LHC, effective field theories, and dark matter candidates in collider searches.

Quantum Field Theory

Higher-order perturbative calculations, resummation techniques, and amplitude methods.

Machine Learning in HEP

Neural networks for event classification, generative models for fast simulation, and anomaly detection.
